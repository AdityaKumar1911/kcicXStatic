Get your Pathways Education Platform connected in 5 minutes

1 Create Supabase Account

If you don't have a Supabase account yet:

Go to Supabase.com
  • Click "Start your project"
  • Sign up with GitHub, Google, or Email
  • It's completely FREE - no credit card required!

2 Create a New Project

  • Click "New Project" in your Supabase dashboard
  • Enter project name: pathways-education
  • Create a strong database password (save it!)
  • Choose a region close to you
  • Click "Create new project"
  • Wait 2-3 minutes for setup to complete

4 Configure Your App

Choose your preferred method:

Option A: Interactive Wizard (Easiest)

Just answer the prompts and you're done!

Option B: Manual Configuration

  • Open the .env file in your project
  • Replace the placeholder values with your credentials
  • Save the file

5 Verify & Start

Verify your configuration:

npm run check-env

Start the development server:
